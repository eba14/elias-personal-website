October 2025

At the DubHacks 2025 hackathon, my team and I created "UW Connections", a personalized student networking and course recommendation website that is designed to help University of Washington students discover peers, study squads, and classes that align with their career goals and interests. This platform connects users based on shared courses, skills, and academic paths, fostering both collaboration and community amongst UW students.

For more information on this project, check out our Devpost.
